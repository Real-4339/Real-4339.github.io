In addition, using it is even more convenient than when we added it fingertip to the biometric sensor. Just look at your smartphone and in a split second you have it unlocked without being obstructed nor an environment with poor lighting conditions.

As for the back of the structure, it resembles an eight for a change. The only thing that has undergone a well-known change here, there is a LED flash next to the main camera, which we will find after it. Everything else, including the protective glass, remained here in its original place.







Ergonomics is due to the round curves and the ideal physical size for me without a single mistake. iPhone XR can be described as a cross between iPhone X and iPhone 8. The front of the structure bears the shape of one model, while the rear side form of the other. Display


As is more or less a tradition, this year we saw a 6.1-inch display called Retina. But after a new word he added another word - Liquid .




The so-called Liquid Retina display consists of advanced LCD technology, which belongs to the first league of the mobile industry. You'll notice it as on bright colors as well as black, because it can confuse you very quickly. In some light conditions, it looks like it is black from more expensive OLED alternative, where the respective pixels are off during display. So it is very close to reality, although it is still backlit .

The manufacturer thus tried to save costs and at the same time not lag too far behind the competition with the Android operating system, where AMOLED displays are a hit this year.




Thanks to this strategy from Apple , the new iPhone XR is irrefutable proof that that the LCD is not yet retiring. With your display capabilities, you don't have to fear or the current flagships from the opposite bank of the river.

True Tone and Wide color

There are two important things behind the great quality of the display. One is True Tone imaging technology, the other again a wide color range . When you combine them into one pile, an environmentally adaptable image is created in a colorful way, which also appeals to the eyes of a more demanding user.

When you're in a dark place, colors are automatically warmer while on a nice sunny day they excel in a cold tone. Behind everything is the manufacturer's effort to reflect reality. Android smartphones can do that, too, but only if we do it manually . which is a bit unnecessarily complicated from a practical point of view. Who would want to adjust it on a daily basis?

The display resolution is somewhere above 300 pixels per inch so nothing special. However, in my humble opinion, no more is needed. The vast majority will no doubt be satisfied with the smoothness of the image potential users. And I can say the same about readability on in direct sunlight or at the lowest level of brightness you get in a dark room you will not be able to praise.

But you will look in vain for the already used touch technology 3D Touch here, because this time it is missing in the hardware. Apple seems to have cut costs in this area as well, and here is the result. But it didn't bother me, because I don't use it at all.

During the day, I would especially highlight the great exposure , including really wide dynamic range. Thanks to him, colors or shadows look in the shots as in reality. Both of them therefore deserve applause, because it raises significantly the quality level of the main camera.

Performance and system


The A12 Bionic chipset present in the bowels is one of the best , what the current mobile industry offers. iOS 12


The operating system iOS 12 brought support for 32-member video call via FaceTime , more detailed management notifications, including their grouping, as well as new functionality to balance our digital life.

But the main thing here is faster startup sub-applications and a temporary increase in computing power in situations where too much demands are required of the smartphone. When the hardware is overloaded, a special one is automatically activated a mode that can double the CPU capacity.

As I mentioned, you can do anything on the iPhone XR however, there will be no situation where you notice any screen jams or inconsistent responses. Such a great synergy of hardware with software, you would probably look in vain elsewhere.




The iOS 12 user interface also deserves praise and its animation or design, which excels in modern minimalism . However, it's not only fast or pretty, but also intuitive , which means that even a technically less capable individual will not have a problem with controlling a smartphone. When he gets used to the missing Home button and new gestures, the simplicity guaranteed not to be able to praise.

Battery Life


It may sound a little weird, but the iPhone XR offers the best endurance among all three new iOS smartphones. Energy efficiency of this model neither XS nor significantly more expensive XS Max is enough. Large battery combination, so the small number of pixels and the energy-efficient chipset is really feel .

My device lasted one full day on a single charge, although I'm one of more demanding users. I called someone here and there, you wrote through Messenger or WhatsApp , surfing the web, listening to music, something here and there photographed or browsed social networks. Simply a classic.




The only thing that bothered me immensely here was the absence of fast-chargers in the base smartphone packaging. When I give so much money for an electronic product, I expect the manufacturer to provide me with proper accessories. However, not for an additional € 75 . Moreover, a similar fate awaits you even when when you want to use wireless charging. The Qi pad still costs something.

Conclusion


Modern looking, top equipped and enormously powerful smartphone for demanding customers. The new iPhone XR has everything it should have every single high-end model this year, including a tuned operating system, which can provide you with a first - class user experience. If you have an iPhone 8 or iPhone 7 , don't hesitate.

If I were to compare the equipment of this model with the iPhone XS, You'll be cut on > OLED display technology, 3D Touch stainless steel construction or dual camera on the back side, but that's about it. However, when you consider it for those approximately € 250 extra costs, so like it. Even with the purchase of a more expensive model, you won't spoil anything .
