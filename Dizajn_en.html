Construction and design


The new iPhone XR looks completely different from the front, what does an eight look like. He got a cut-out display and it lacks a hardware Home button with which the Touch ID has disappeared.

The user only has a Face ID , ie face recognition, which, however, does not matter at all. On the contrary, it works perfectly. In addition, using it is even more convenient than when we added it fingertip to the biometric sensor. Just look at your smartphone and in a split second you have it unlocked without being obstructed nor an environment with poor lighting conditions.

As for the back of the structure, it resembles an eight for a change. The only thing that has undergone a well-known change here, there is a LED flash next to the main camera, which we will find after it. Everything else, including the protective glass, remained here in its original place.







Ergonomics is due to the round curves and the ideal physical size for me without a single mistake. Even the one to whom they were will not complain about the dimensions palms or fingers of medium height. This iOS smartphone is holding up really great. You just have to get used to the clearly visible fingerprints and slippery body, which, on the other hand, can solve the protective case ..

Built-in stereo speakers excel at extreme volume and great the purity of reproduction that we've become accustomed to with Apple smartphones over the years.

iPhone XR can be described as a cross between iPhone X and iPhone 8. The front of the structure bears the shape of one model, while the rear side form of the other. But one important thing has nothing to do with none mentioned model. This is a display panel, which we will now look at a little closer.


Display


As is more or less a tradition, this year we saw a 6.1-inch display called Retina. But after a new word he added another word - Liquid .




The so-called Liquid Retina display consists of advanced LCD technology, which belongs to the first league of the mobile industry. You'll notice it as on bright colors as well as black, because it can confuse you very quickly. In some light conditions, it looks like it is black from more expensive OLED alternative, where the respective pixels are off during display. So it is very close to reality, although it is still backlit .

The manufacturer thus tried to save costs and at the same time not lag too far behind the competition with the Android operating system, where AMOLED displays are a hit this year.




Thanks to this strategy from Apple , the new iPhone XR is irrefutable proof that that the LCD is not yet retiring. With your display capabilities, you don't have to fear or the current flagships from the opposite bank of the river.

True Tone and Wide color

There are two important things behind the great quality of the display. One is True Tone imaging technology, the other again a wide color range . When you combine them into one pile, an environmentally adaptable image is created in a colorful way, which also appeals to the eyes of a more demanding user.

When you're in a dark place, colors are automatically warmer while on a nice sunny day they excel in a cold tone. Behind everything is the manufacturer's effort to reflect reality. Android smartphones can do that, too, but only if we do it manually . which is a bit unnecessarily complicated from a practical point of view. Who would want to adjust it on a daily basis?

The display resolution is somewhere above 300 pixels per inch so nothing special. However, in my humble opinion, no more is needed. The vast majority will no doubt be satisfied with the smoothness of the image potential users. And I can say the same about readability on in direct sunlight or at the lowest level of brightness you get in a dark room you will not be able to praise.

But you will look in vain for the already used touch technology 3D Touch here, because this time it is missing in the hardware. Apple seems to have cut costs in this area as well, and here is the result. But it didn't bother me, because I don't use it at all.


Cameras


You'll only find one camera on the back of the iPhone XR. The one that stays there offers a wide-angle view and also has great brightness. In addition to the f / 1.8 aperture or peak optical stabilization received even relatively large pixels, which reach 1.4 µm. This combination is will make sure that there is enough light in the photo with almost imperceptible noise. And where there is a lot of light, there is logically a lot of detail.

During the day, I would especially like to highlight the great exposure, including properly wide dynamic range. Thanks to him, colors or shadows look in the shots as in reality. Both of them therefore deserve applause, because it raises significantly the quality level of the main camera.



Experts from the DxOMark server also write about it, where they mention the iPhone XS Max, but both smartphones got the same camera with a wide angle view. The XR only lacks the one with the telephoto zoom lens without loss of quality, which we will especially appreciate for portraits. Therefore, you can safely proceed from this related review. The tested iOS novelty is still missing in the database and thus also in the relevant ranking.

In addition to the great exposure or wide dynamic range, it is also worth a compliment also autofocus. In addition to being accurate, it's also lightning fast. When you press the shutter button, you take a first-class shot in a fraction of a second, which is basically more beautiful than a shot from the iPhone 8. Even though that the specification remained more or less unchanged.

Apple engineers have focused much of their software innovation this year page of the main camera, thanks to which they managed to increase the quality as well without significant interference with hardware numbers. The user therefore has it available the same number of megapixels and the same aperture number.




The charm here lies in the newly added Smart HDR functionality, served by artificial intelligence. Machine learning selects the images taken, to put them together and create one high quality photo. But the most important thing here is that the selection is also waiting for individual shots, not just the complex one. This handy trick will visually enrich the shadows and in too shiny places, a little more detail can be seen again.

Video

However, the camera did not disappoint me either. If you need a smartphone that can make quality videos, So the iPhone XR may be one of the potential options. Rich details, again a wide dynamic range, perfectly functioning optical stabilization and even perfect autofocus will make sure you get out of this news premium looking video footage.

Selfie

But don't expect another quality standard from the front camera either, where they built a 7 Mpx sensor with an f / 2.2 aperture and an improved electronic image stabilization. The first two things remained unchanged, but that was it on the other hand, it does not immediately mean that there is nothing to praise.

If you are a so-called self-maniac, you will definitely come to your own. Especially thanks to the pre-prepared studio lights or a natural-looking blurred background when taking portraits. Both give them a professional flair. When you find the right angle and you are in an environment with great lighting conditions, you can make a surprisingly nice selfie.

iOS 12


The iOS 12 operating system brought support for 32-member video call via FaceTime, more detailed management notifications, including their grouping, as well as new functionality to balance our digital life.

But the main thing here is a faster launch sub-applications and a temporary increase in computing power in situations where too much demands are required of the smartphone. When the hardware is overloaded, a special one is automatically activated a mode that can double the capacity of the processor.

As I mentioned, you can do anything on the iPhone XR, however, there will be no situation where you notice any screen jams or inconsistent responses. So great hardware synergy with software, you would probably look in vain elsewhere.




The iOS 12 user interface also deserves praise and its animation or design, which excels in modern minimalism. However, it is not only fast or nice, but also intuitive, which means that even a technically less capable individual will not have a problem with controlling a smartphone. When he gets used to the missing Home button and new gestures, that's the simplicity guaranteed not to be able to praise.

Conclusion

However, when you consider it for those approximately € 250 extra costs, so like it. Even with the purchase of a more expensive model, you won't spoil anything.
